  • The 3 locations I found were technology, bench, and punchdrunk.
  • The location that we skipped was pumpitup.

How I search for the places:

  1. Use Google Lens to reverse image search on special features of the image.
  2. Go to "Visual matches" & look for correct results.
  3. Find the corresponding places on Google Maps.
